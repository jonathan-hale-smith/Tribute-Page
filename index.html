John James Audubon & Lucy Blakewell Audubon

together, he became the standard

Upper portrait: Lucy Blakewell
Lower portrait: John James Audubon

  • April 26, 1785 - Birth of Jean Rabin in Haiti, which was then a French Colony. Father was a Navy General.
  • January 18, 1787 - Lucy Blakewell is born in England to a wealthy family
  • 1808 - Jean Rabin (now known by John James Audubon) and Lucy Blakewell are married
  • 1808 -> 1826 - James leads the family through a series of failed business ventures, saved only by his wife's business acumen and teaching skills
  • 1826 - James heads to England, finally selling his drawings of American birds and launching his fame as a leading ornithologist.
